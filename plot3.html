Homework 9: Interactive story

Then college started. Life became louder, faster, more demanding—but you didn’t want to let go of Sky. It had become a place of comfort, a soft escape you clung to between assignments and lectures. So you kept logging in, sometimes late at night, just to glide through the skies and feel the peace the game always brought you. But slowly, things began to change. your friends—the ones you used to fly with—stopped showing up. Their candles no longer lit up the home space. Days passed, then weeks, and you found yourself wandering the realms alone. The world was still beautiful, still serene, but lonelier now. you stayed, hoping they’d return. you still played, but the silence felt heavier than before.
