SKY JOURNAL

The game gently guided you with glowing symbols and visual cues, teaching you how to fly, light candles, and unlock new paths. The tutorials were subtle but helpful, easing you into the rhythm of exploration. you wandered through lush meadows, golden deserts, and mysterious, fog-covered forests, each realm more breathtaking than the last. Along the way, you collected shimmering winged lights and glowing emotes, slowly building your strength and style. But the real magic was in the people you met—silent figures who offered candles, gestures of friendship, and companionship in a world where words weren’t needed. you guys flew together, helped each other through challenges, and shared quiet moments beneath glowing skies. Sky wasn’t just a game—it felt like a peaceful escape, a gentle world that welcomed you with open arms.
