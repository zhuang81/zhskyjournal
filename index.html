Homework 9: Interactive Story

At first, you found yourself standing alone in a glowing meadow, wrapped in golden light and silence. Unsure of what to do, you started walking, then running, then gliding across soft hills and strange floating structures. There were no words, no instructions—just the wind, the sky, and flickering lights that seemed to beckon. you chased after shadows of other players, lit candles, and followed trails of stars through mysterious temples and ancient ruins. The world was beautiful, peaceful even—but the silence soon began to feel empty. you explored for a while longer, hoping something would click, but the lack of guidance left you feeling lost in a dream you didn’t quite understand.
